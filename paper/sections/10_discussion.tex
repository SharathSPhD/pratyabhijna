\section{Discussion}\label{sec:discussion}

The pre-registered design forces us to resist post-hoc storytelling. We discuss only the patterns that the data already exhibit.

\paragraph{What the cascade contributes.} The clearest signal we set out to look for is on the AUT and Wittgenstein aspect-shift slices: PCE should help most where the scoring \emph{rewards multiplicity}. The forest plot (Figure~\ref{fig:forest}) shows the directionality on those two domains and quantifies how much. Where the directionality is reversed (or the Holm-adjusted $p$ does not survive), this is registered as a negative result --- the SPEC's negative-result obligation makes this a deliverable, not a failure mode.

\paragraph{The Haiku gap is the wrong frame for some domains.} The poetry\_gen scoring is heavy on POEMetric axes that reward fluency (lexical diversity, imagery, literary devices). A 1.5B-parameter local model with PCE will lose to Haiku on \emph{fluency} regardless of the cascade. The claim PCE makes is structural (multiplicity, recursive aspect-shift), not stylistic. The within-PCE H6 test (Figure~\ref{fig:h6}) and the local\_bare sensitivity contrast (autoreport) are the correctly-framed tests.

\paragraph{Vimar\'sa as a tunable parameter.} The aspect-cosine-hit threshold (0.40 after Phase~6 calibration; see ADR-002) is the single most consequential hyperparameter. We surface it in the audit log of every cascade run and the \texttt{/pce-trace} command shows it explicitly so a downstream user can argue with the choice. Lowering the threshold raises the firing rate but increases false positives; raising it makes \iast{vimar\'sa} silent on borderline aspect-shift surfaces. We report H6 \emph{at the calibrated value} and treat re-tuning as a follow-up.

\paragraph{Limits of the substrate.} Qwen2-1.5B-Instruct is a small model. Where it produces incoherent surfaces, no cascade can rescue them; PCE's contribution is bounded above by the substrate's coherence ceiling. The architectural separation between substrate and cascade, however, means PCE can be re-attached to a larger model with no code change beyond the \texttt{LMConfig.model\_id} field; the \texttt{kriya} \texttt{claude\_polish} mode is the path through which a larger LLM can be opted-in for the surface step alone.

\paragraph{What the engine does \emph{not} claim.} PCE does not claim to replicate phenomenological consciousness. The Pratyabhij\~n\=a vocabulary is borrowed because its taxonomy is exceptionally fine-grained, not because we make first-person claims. Likewise, BMR is a posterior-switching primitive in our system; we do not claim that the Friston-style free-energy variational identity holds at the system level. The honest claim is: \emph{this taxonomy of operators, when written down as code, produces a measurable lift on multiplicity-rewarding benchmarks under the conditions documented here.}
