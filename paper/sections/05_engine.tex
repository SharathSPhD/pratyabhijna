\section{The Pratyabhij\~n\=a Creative Engine}\label{sec:engine}

The engine is a deterministic pipeline of seven operators plus two sleep-style consolidation cycles, all implemented in \texttt{src/pce/}. We summarise each operator's signature here and refer to Appendix~\ref{app:operator_specs} for the full equations and Appendix~\ref{app:consolidation} for the consolidation cycles.

\subsection{Substrate}
Two substrate primitives ground the cascade in real computation: a sentence-embedder $\phi_{\text{embed}}$ (\texttt{sentence-transformers/all-MiniLM-L6-v2}, $d=384$) and a local causal LM $\pi_{\text{LM}}$ (\texttt{Qwen/Qwen2-1.5B-Instruct}). The latter is loaded with autodetected device (CUDA $\rightarrow$ MPS $\rightarrow$ CPU) and \texttt{float16} precision on accelerated devices. Sampling is deterministic given a seed.

\subsection{Operators}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.2em]
\item $\textsc{Cit}(\text{prompt}, \tau, \text{seed}) \to (\text{tokens}, \log p, \text{embedding})$. Direct call to $\pi_{\text{LM}}.\text{generate}$.
\item $\textsc{\=Ananda}(\text{candidate}, \text{constraint}, \text{retrieval-set}) \to r \in [0,1]$. A weighted aggregate of (i) constraint-cosine, (ii) retrieval-set diversity, (iii) form-fidelity heuristics (line / syllable proxies for poetry).
\item $\textsc{Icch\=a}(\text{prompt}, K, \text{constraint}) \to \{c_1, \dots, c_K\}$. $K$ candidate generations under a constraint-injected prompt; each $c_i$ carries its own (tokens, embedding, $\log p$).
\item $\textsc{Apohana}(\{c_i\}, \text{must-avoid}) \to \{a_i\}$. Contrastive embedding score: $a_i = 1 - \max_j \cos(\phi(c_i), \phi(\text{must-avoid}_j))$.
\item $\textsc{J\~n\=ana}(\{c_i, a_i\}) \to (i^*, \Delta F)$. BMR over a Dirichlet--multinomial whose pseudo-counts are scaled by $a_i$. The selected $i^*$ is the posterior maximum; $\Delta F$ is recorded for the audit log.
\item $\textsc{Kriy\=a}(c_{i^*}, \text{mode}) \to \text{surface}$. Three modes: \texttt{verbatim} (return the candidate unchanged), \texttt{polish} (a deterministic detokenisation with whitespace normalisation), \texttt{claude\_polish} (Claude Code's editing surface, opt-in).
\item $\textsc{Vimar\'sa}(\text{surface}, \text{aspects}, \text{retrieval-set}) \to (\text{event}, \text{novelty})$. Fires if any of (a) aspect-multiplicity (multiple aspect-targets each cosine $\geq 0.40$), (b) low-retrieval-novelty, (c) negative residual $\Delta F$.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Cascade}
The cascade in \texttt{src/pce/cascade.py} composes the seven operators in order. The state object \texttt{CascadeState} records all intermediates so the audit log can reconstruct any decision. The cascade is deterministic: identical inputs and seed produce byte-identical outputs.

\subsection{Consolidation}
Two cycles operate over the Hopfield store: \textsc{ConsolidateSWS} (k-means with k chosen by elbow-statistic) for slow-wave-sleep abstraction; \textsc{ConsolidateREM} (Metropolis re-sampling that perturbs and re-stores) for rare-attractor maintenance. Both cycles are exposed as MCP tools so a downstream agent can request consolidation explicitly.
