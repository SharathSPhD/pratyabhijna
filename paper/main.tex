\begin{abstract}
We introduce the \emph{Pratyabhij\~n\=a Creative Engine} (PCE), a Claude Code plugin that operationalises Abhinavagupta's recognitional architecture (the five-\iast{\'sakti} cascade and the recursive \iast{vimar\'sa} loop) as an executable layer over an LLM, coupling each \iast{\'sakti}-operator to a concrete active-inference primitive: a temperature-tunable generative substrate (\iast{cit}), a constraint-aware aesthetic-coherence scoring head (\iast{\=ananda}), a candidate sampler (\iast{icch\=a}), an embedding-space contrastive exclusion operator (\iast{apohana}-driven \iast{j\~n\=ana}), free-energy posterior selection via Bayesian Model Reduction \citep{Friston2018BayesianModelReductionArxiv,FristonPenny2011}, surface enaction (\iast{kriy\=a}), and a recursive aspect-shift detector (\iast{vimar\'sa}). PCE additionally exposes a Hopfield-attractor \iast{\=alayavij\~n\=ana} with SWS/REM consolidation cycles. We pre-register six hypotheses (H1--H6) covering CreativityPrism alternative-uses-task aggregate, Wittgenstein aspect-shift poetry interpretation, POEMetric advanced-creative-abilities composite for poetry generation, scientific-creativity items from BBH, an aggregate composite, and a within-condition test of whether the \iast{vimar\'sa} loop itself causally drives the lift. Across $n_{\text{paired}}={38}$ paired observations we report effect sizes, paired permutation $p$-values, BCa bootstrap 95\% CIs, Wilcoxon signed-rank $p$-values, Holm--Bonferroni correction, and a-priori + retrospective power. We also report a sensitivity arm (PCE on Local-Qwen vs Local-Qwen alone) that isolates the contribution of the cascade independently of the underlying LLM. The plugin, all benchmark items, generated outputs, raw scores, and statistical tests are published as artefacts; every numerical claim in this paper is generated from those artefacts and traceable through \texttt{audit/phase9/}. \textbf{Headline result.} {No primary hypothesis (H1--H4) crosses the pre-registered Holm-adjusted $p<0.05$ threshold against no-PCE Haiku --- a negative result we report in compliance with the SPEC's negative-result obligation.}
\end{abstract}
