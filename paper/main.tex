\begin{abstract}
We introduce the \emph{Pratyabhij\~n\=a Creative Engine} (PCE), a Claude Code plugin that operationalises Abhinavagupta's recognitional architecture (the five-\iast{\'sakti} cascade and the recursive \iast{vimar\'sa} loop) as an executable layer over an LLM, coupling each \iast{\'sakti}-operator to a concrete active-inference primitive: a temperature-tunable generative substrate (\iast{cit}), a constraint-aware aesthetic-coherence scoring head (\iast{\=ananda}), a candidate sampler (\iast{icch\=a}), an embedding-space contrastive exclusion operator (\iast{apohana}-driven \iast{j\~n\=ana}), free-energy posterior selection via Bayesian Model Reduction \citep{Friston2018BayesianModelReductionArxiv,FristonPenny2011}, surface enaction (\iast{kriy\=a}), and a recursive aspect-shift detector (\iast{vimar\'sa}). PCE additionally exposes a Hopfield-attractor \iast{\=alayavij\~n\=ana} with SWS/REM consolidation cycles. \textbf{This paper reports v0.2}, which makes \iast{vimar\'sa} causal via a two-pass-always revision pipeline (every cascade item now goes draft $\to$ \iast{vimar\'sa} brief $\to$ revision); adds Anthropic's Claude Haiku as a first-class generative substrate alongside the local Qwen2-1.5B-Instruct; resolves five operator-level contradictions identified in the v0.1 adversarial review using TRIZ contradiction analysis (signed \iast{apohana}, prompt/sampler parity with bare baselines, two-pass-always reflection cap, pluggable LM protocol, per-operator sampler profiles); and runs a four-arm pilot benchmark (\texttt{local\_bare}, \texttt{local\_cascade}, \texttt{haiku\_bare}, \texttt{haiku\_cascade}) across {19} paired items in four creativity domains. We pre-register six hypotheses (H1--H6) covering AUT/CreativityPrism, Wittgenstein aspect-shift poetry interpretation, POEMetric poetry generation, BBH-style scientific creativity, an aggregate composite, and a within-cascade \iast{vimar\'sa}-fired-vs-not test. We report paired effect sizes, BCa bootstrap 95\% CIs, exact sign-flip permutation tests, Wilcoxon signed-rank $p$-values, Holm--Bonferroni correction, and both a-priori and retrospective power. The plugin, all benchmark items, generated outputs, raw scores, and statistical tests are published as artefacts; every numerical claim in this paper is generated from those artefacts and traceable through \texttt{audit/}. \textbf{Headline result.} {Effect sizes for H1, H2, H4 are positive with 95\% BCa CIs strictly above zero in the \texttt{haiku\_cascade} vs.\ \texttt{haiku\_bare} contrast, but the pre-registered Holm-adjusted $p<0.05$ threshold is not crossed at the pilot's $n$=5/domain (the exact sign-flip permutation floor is $1/2^{5}=0.0312$ and Holm with $m{=}4$ pushes the smallest possible adjusted $p$ to $0.125$). The within-cascade H6 test points in the predicted direction ($\Delta=+0.031$, $n_{\text{fired}}=9$) but does not reach the significance threshold at the pilot's $n$.}
\end{abstract}
