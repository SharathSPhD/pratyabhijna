# Gāyatrī to Citi-śakti

**cit-śakti-sar-va-kā-ri-māṃ |**
**jña-na-lo-ka-pra-dī-pi-nī |**
**bud-dhiṃ naḥ pra-chō-da-yā-tu ||**

---

## Structure
- **Pāda 1** (8 syllables): The all-working consciousness-power
- **Pāda 2** (8 syllables): Illuminator of the knowledge-realm  
- **Pāda 3** (8 syllables): May she awaken our wisdom

## Translation
*O Citi-śakti, omnipresent power,*  
*Illuminator of the realm of knowing,*  
*Awaken our wisdom, we pray.*

---

This gāyatrī follows the traditional three-pāda structure (8+8+8=24 syllables) in the conventional form established by the Gāyatrī Mantra itself, addressing the luminous power of consciousness (*citi-śakti*) as the clarifier of cognition, with a closing prayer in the optative mood characteristic of gāyatrī composition.
